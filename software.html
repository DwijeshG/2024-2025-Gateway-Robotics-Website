What are the Responsibilities of Software Team

The software team is the life of the robot. Without them, we would not have been able to have participated in the main event. They code the robot and make sure it works. They also help our build team design the robot. When the robot was tested, if we ran into a problem, the software team would debug and figure out what went wrong. The software team is one of the most important, and I can't stress enough how smart and clever they are at creating the code. None of this would be possible without the Software Team!
