What are the Responsibilities of Marketing Team

The marketing team's main goal is to create and promote our company identity while selling the robot the team builds. They present the robot to judges and create slideshows to showcase its features. Additionally, the marketing team handles important tasks like designing the team T-shirt, conducting interviews, taking pictures of the team in action, and organizing fundraisers. They also gather feedback from people to understand how the team is perceived. By managing these responsibilities, they ensure the team is represented professionally and effectively. Their work allows other teams to focus on their core tasks without worrying about promotions or presentations. Ultimately, the marketing team plays a crucial role in building awareness and creating a positive image for the team.
