What are the Responsibilities of Exhibit team

The Exhibit team in the Gateways robotics team has important responsibilities in promoting the program. They reach out to other communities to share what makes the Gateways robotics team special and exciting. By creating enthusiasm, they encourage more people to get involved in robotics and STEM. The team also builds the exhibit, making sure it's appealing. Their displays aim to draw in visitors and spark interest. They provide clear information about everything the team does, helping others understand the world of robotics. Overall, the Exhibit team helps raise awareness of the Gateways robotics program and brings people together.
