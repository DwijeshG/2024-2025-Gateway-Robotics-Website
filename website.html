The Gateway High School Robotics Website team consists of Dwijesh Gontla (Captain), Vidip Koppuravari (Co-Captain), Brendan Jones (Scribe), Gaman Siddhanth Potlacheruvu, Venkata Sai Ruthvik Bommisetty, and Aidan Herron. We used the last year's website as a guide and followed the scoresheet to meet all the requirements.
